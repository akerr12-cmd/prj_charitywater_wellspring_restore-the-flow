# HOW TO PLAY  

*Restore the broken water pipeline and uncover the well.*

---

## 🎯 GOAL

Clear obstacles, build functional card sequences, uncover the hidden **Well Card**, and place the final Solution to restore clean water to the community.

---

# 🃏 CARD TYPES

## **Tools (T)**

Used to prepare or weaken Challenges.  
Examples: Rope, Pipe, Wrench.

### **Challenges (C)**

Obstacles blocking the pipeline.  
Examples: Drought, Broken Pump, Contamination.

### **Solutions (S)**

Clear Challenges and unlock hidden layers.  
Examples: New Pump, Pipe Replacement.

### **Impact Cards (I)**

Boost score, reveal hidden cards, or grant bonuses.  
Examples: Clean Water Reached 50 People.

---

# 🧱 GAME LAYOUT

## **Tableau**

- 8 columns of layered cards  
- Some cards are hidden or locked behind Challenges  

### **Water Tanks**

- 4–6 temporary storage slots  
- Hold cards while reorganizing sequences  

### **Foundations**

Build four piles:

1. Tools  
2. Solutions  
3. Challenges Cleared  
4. Impact Cards  

All must be completed to win.

---

# 🔗 VALID SEQUENCES

You may stack cards in these orders:

## ✔ **Tool → Tool**  

Builds a tool chain.

### ✔ **Tool → Solution**  

Prepares a Solution play.

### ✔ **Challenge → Solution**  

Clears the Challenge.

### ✔ **Tool → Challenge → Solution**  

Advanced combo.

### ✔ **Solution → Impact**  

Creates a scoring combo.

### ✔ **Impact → Impact**  

Stacks multipliers.

---

# ❌ INVALID SEQUENCES

These cannot be played:

- Challenge → Challenge  
- Impact → Challenge  
- Solution → Tool  
- Impact → Tool  
- Solution → Challenge (wrong type)  

---

# 🧩 CLEARING CHALLENGES

Each Challenge requires a specific Solution:

- **Drought** → Emergency Water Delivery  
- **Broken Pump** → New Pump  
- **Contamination** → Anti‑Contamination Protocol  
- **Mudslide** → Well Cleaning Crew  
- **Heatwave** → Solar Panel Repair  
- **Rusted Pipe** → Pipe Replacement  
- **Water Theft** → Community Training  
- **Supply Delay** → Pressure Regulator  
- **Low Water Pressure** → Pressure Regulator  
- **Blocked Well** → Leak Seal Operation  
- **Clogged Intake** → Intake Repair Team  
- **Pipe Burst** → Leak Seal Operation  

Clearing a Challenge:

- Unlocks hidden cards  
- Removes penalties  
- Advances the “Challenges Cleared” foundation  

---

# 🧱 LAYER‑CLEARING

Some cards are:

- Face‑down  
- Locked behind Challenges  
- Buried under other cards  

To reveal them:

- Clear the Challenge above  
- Apply the correct Solution  
- Use Tools to weaken obstacles  
- Play Impact Cards that reveal layers  

---

# ⚠️ TWIST MECHANICS

## **Contamination Spread**

Locks 2 columns until solved.

### **Heatwave**

Temporarily reduces available moves.

### **Water Theft**

Steals an Impact Card unless countered.

### **Blocked Well**

Prevents access to the final Well Card.

### **Storage Limits**

- Water Tanks are limited  
- Some cards cannot be stored  
- Some Solutions weaken if stored too long  

---

# 🎉 WIN CONDITION

To win, you must:

1. Clear all Challenges  
2. Complete all four Foundations  
3. Uncover the **Well Card**  
4. Place the final Solution on the Well Card  

This triggers:

- Water splash animation  
- Confetti  
- “Clean Water Restored!” message  
- Transition to the Impact Screen  

---

# 📊 IMPACT SCREEN

After winning, you’ll see:

- Final Score  
- Moves Taken  
- Challenges Cleared  
- Tools Used  
- Solutions Applied  
- Impact Cards Earned  
- People Helped  
- Time Spent  
- Return to Main Menu  

---

# 💡 TIPS

- Use Water Tanks wisely — they fill up fast.  
- Clear Challenges early to avoid penalties.  
- Impact Cards are powerful; chain them for big scores.  
- Look ahead before committing to a sequence.  
- The Well Card is always buried deep — plan for it.
