How to Play

Goal

Restore the community well to 100% by organizing cards into functional sequences, clearing challenges, and completing all four foundation piles. When the final Solution is placed on the Well Card, clean water is fully restored.

Card Types

Tool

Equipment used to prepare repairs (Rope, Pipe, Wrench, Filter, etc.). Tools often start sequences.

Challenge

Obstacles blocking progress (Drought, Broken Pump, Contamination). These must be cleared with the correct Solution.

Solution

Fixes specific Challenges and advances the restoration effort. Each Challenge has exactly one matching Solution.

Impact

Represents real-world change. Playing Impact cards boosts score, increases “people helped,” and can chain for big bonuses.

Board Layout

  • 8 Tableau Columns — Cards stack downward. Only the top card of each column can be moved.
  • Water Tanks — Temporary storage slots (like FreeCells). Hold one card each.
  • Foundations — Four piles: Tools, Solutions, Cleared Challenges, and Impact.
  • Well Card — Hidden deep in the tableau. Revealing it is required to win.

Valid Sequences

Cards can be stacked in the tableau only if they follow the pipeline logic:

  • Tool → Tool — Build a tool chain.
  • Tool → Solution — A prepared solution.
  • Tool → Challenge — (Optional prep step, depending on the column.)
  • Challenge → Solution — Clears the Challenge.
  • Solution → Impact — Creates a scoring combo.
  • Impact → Impact — Stack multipliers.

Invalid sequences (e.g., Challenge → Challenge, Solution → Tool, Impact → Challenge) cannot be placed.

Clearing Challenges

Each Challenge has a matching Solution. When placed correctly, the Challenge is cleared, hidden cards are revealed, and progress increases.

  • Drought → Emergency Water Delivery
  • Broken Pump → New Pump
  • Contamination → Anti-Contamination Protocol
  • Mudslide → Well Cleaning Crew
  • Heatwave → Solar Panel Repair
  • Rusted Pipe → Pipe Replacement
  • Water Theft → Community Training
  • Supply Delay → Pressure Regulator
  • Low Water Pressure → Flow Stabilizer
  • Blocked Well → Leak Seal Operation
  • Clogged Intake → Intake Repair Team
  • Pipe Burst → High-Pressure Seal

Impact Cards

Impact cards represent the real-world effects of clean water. Playing them:

  • Adds score
  • Increases “people helped”
  • Triggers multipliers when chained

Water Tanks (Free Cells)

Use Water Tanks to temporarily store cards while reorganizing the tableau. Each tank holds one card. Use them wisely — they fill up fast.

Foundations

Build four foundation piles to complete the restoration:

  • Tools Foundation — All tool cards.
  • Solutions Foundation — All solution cards.
  • Cleared Challenges — Automatically fills as you solve challenges.
  • Impact Foundation — All impact cards.

Twist Mechanics ⚠️

Some Challenges trigger special effects when revealed or moved:

  • Contamination Spread — Shuffles random cards.
  • Pipe Lock — Locks a card for several seconds.
  • Heatwave — Speeds up the timer.
  • Blocked Well — Prevents final restoration until cleared.

Winning the Game

To win, you must:

  • Clear all Challenges
  • Complete all four Foundations
  • Reveal the hidden Well Card
  • Place the final Solution on the Well Card

This triggers the celebration screen, confetti, water splash animation, and your final impact report.
