How to Play

Goal

Restore the community well to 100% by flipping cards and making functional matches before the timer runs out.

Card Types

Tool

Physical items needed to repair the well (Wrench, Pipe, Filter, Rope).

Solution

Resources and actions that fix problems and restore the well.

Challenge

Obstacles that must be overcome. Mismatching two of these costs points!

Impact

Matching an Impact pair activates a 2× score multiplier and adds 50 people helped!

Matching Rules

  • Tool + Solution → +15% Well Progress
  • Challenge + Solution → +10% Well Progress & removes the obstacle
  • Impact + Impact → +10% Progress & 2× multiplier
  • Challenge + Challenge → −100 pts penalty + twist effects!

Twist Mechanics ⚠️

  • Contamination Spread — shuffles 3 random face-down cards.
  • Broken Pipe — locks a random card for 5 seconds.
  • Heatwave — the timer speeds up for 15 seconds!

Skip

Flip one card, then press Skip to cancel the turn. You have 3 skips per game. Each skip deducts 25 pts from your final score.
